Common Diseases - Guillain-Barré Syndrome (GBS)

Symptoms

  • Weakness in legs
  • Tingling sensations
  • Difficulty walking
  • Loss of reflexes
  • Breathing issues

Symptoms to Not Ignore

  • Rapid paralysis
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Heart rate fluctuations
  • Severe pain

Ayurvedic Remedies

  • Ashwagandha for nerve health
  • Brahmi for brain and nervous system support
  • Turmeric for inflammation reduction

Normal Remedies

  • Plasma exchange therapy
  • Intravenous immunoglobulin (IVIG)
  • Physiotherapy

Eating Tips

  • High-protein diet for muscle repair (chicken, fish, lentils)
  • Foods rich in omega-3 (flaxseeds, walnuts) for nerve health
  • Plenty of fluids to maintain hydration

Healthy Fruits

  • Bananas for potassium
  • Blueberries for antioxidants
  • Avocado for healthy fats

Yoga Activities

  • Pranayama (deep breathing) for lung function
  • Gentle stretching to maintain flexibility

Physical and Mental Exercises

  • Physical therapy exercises for muscle strength
  • Meditation to reduce stress and support recovery

Holistic Approach

  • Early treatment is crucial
  • Regular physiotherapy
  • A balanced diet
  • Stress management

Quick Statistics

Duration: Recovery can take weeks to months; severe cases may take years

Medicine Cost: ₹50,000-₹5,00,000 for immunotherapy, physiotherapy, and hospital care

Prevalence: Affects about 1-2 people per 100,000 annually; can occur at any age

Consequences: Nerve damage, muscle weakness, long-term mobility issues
