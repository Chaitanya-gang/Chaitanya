Common Diseases - Cystic Fibrosis

Symptoms

  • Persistent cough
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Frequent lung infections

Symptoms to Not Ignore

  • Severe respiratory symptoms
  • Failure to gain weight

Ayurvedic Remedies

  • Tulsi (Holy Basil) for respiratory health
  • Turmeric Milk to reduce inflammation
  • Ashwagandha for energy and immunity
  • Licorice Root (Mulethi) for soothing throat irritation
  • Ginger & Honey Mixture as a natural expectorant

Normal Remedies

  • Enzyme replacement therapy
  • Consistent adherence to treatment plans

Eating Tips

  • High-calorie diet for energy needs
  • Adequate hydration to thin mucus
  • Increased sodium intake if required

Healthy Fruits

  • Avocado for healthy fats
  • Bananas and mangoes for caloric density

Yoga Activities

  • Pranayama to improve lung capacity
  • Gentle stretching exercises

Physical and Mental Exercises

  • Regular aerobic exercise
  • Group therapy sessions for emotional well-being

Quick Statistics:

Duration: Lifelong condition

Medicine Cost: ₹30,000 - ₹1,00,000 per month

Prevalence: 1 in 2,500 to 3,500 births in India

Consequences: Can significantly impact quality and length of life
