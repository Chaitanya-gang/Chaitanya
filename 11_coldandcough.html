Common Diseases - Cold and Cough

Symptoms

  • Runny nose
  • Sneezing
  • Sore throat
  • Mild fever
  • Fatigue

Symptoms to Not Ignore

  • Severe headache
  • High fever (over 39°C)
  • Shortness of breath

Ayurvedic Remedies

  • Tulsi tea
  • Ginger with honey
  • Steam inhalation with eucalyptus oil

Normal Remedies

  • Rest
  • Hydration
  • Paracetamol for fever

Eating Tips

  • Consume warm, nutritious soups and broths
  • Include foods high in vitamin C (like oranges, guavas) and zinc (like nuts, seeds)
  • Stay hydrated with herbal teas and warm water

Healthy Fruits

  • Citrus fruits (oranges, lemons)
  • Berries (strawberries, blueberries)
  • Kiwi and guava

Yoga Activities

  • Anulom Vilom (alternate nostril breathing)
  • Cat-Cow pose (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)
  • Gentle stretches and forward bends to relieve upper respiratory pressure

Physical and Mental Exercises

  • Light aerobic activities (walking, jogging)
  • Mindfulness meditation or deep breathing exercises

Holistic Approach

  • Warm water, salt water gargles
  • Avoid cold foods

Quick Statistics

Duration: 7-10 days

Medicine Cost: ₹200 - ₹1,000

Prevalence: Affects millions annually; approximately 20-30% of adults in India experience colds each year; equal impact on males and females

Consequences: Weakening of the immune system, potential for secondary infections
